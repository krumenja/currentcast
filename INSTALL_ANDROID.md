# Installing CurrentCast on Your Android Phone

CurrentCast is a free fishing app that isn't on the Google Play Store — it's distributed directly as an APK file. Installing it takes about 5 minutes and only requires changing one phone setting.

---

## Step 1 — Download the APK

1. On your Android phone, open Chrome (or any browser)
2. Go to the CurrentCast GitHub page: **[github.com/krumenja/currentcast](https://github.com/krumenja/currentcast)**
3. Tap **Releases** on the right side of the page
4. Under the latest release, tap **CurrentCast.apk** to download it

> If you're on a computer, download the APK and transfer it to your phone via USB cable, Google Drive, or email attachment.

---

## Step 2 — Allow installation from unknown sources

Because CurrentCast isn't on the Play Store, Android needs your permission to install it. This is a one-time setting.

**Android 8 and newer (most phones):**
1. When you tap the APK to install, Android will ask if you want to allow your file manager to install unknown apps
2. Tap **Settings** in that prompt, then toggle **Allow from this source** ON
3. Go back and tap **Install**

**If Android doesn't prompt you automatically:**
1. Open **Settings** on your phone
2. Go to **Apps** → **Special app access** → **Install unknown apps**
3. Select your file manager or browser and toggle it ON
4. Then open the APK file from your Downloads folder

---

## Step 3 — Install

1. Open your **Downloads** folder (or wherever the APK saved)
2. Tap **CurrentCast.apk**
3. Tap **Install**
4. Android may show a warning like *"App not verified"* — this is normal for apps outside the Play Store. Tap **Install anyway** or **More details → Install**
5. Tap **Open** when installation finishes

The CurrentCast icon will appear in your app drawer.

---

## Step 4 — First launch

When you open the app for the first time:

- The app will ask for **location permission** — tap **Allow** to use GPS. You can also search any location manually if you prefer not to share your location.
- The map will center on your current position and load the local forecast automatically.

---

## Updating the app

When a new version is released, just download the new APK and install it over the existing app — your saved fishing spots and catch log will be preserved.

---

## Uninstalling

Same as any app: long-press the CurrentCast icon and tap **Uninstall**, or go to **Settings → Apps → CurrentCast → Uninstall**.

---

## Troubleshooting

**The APK won't install — "App not installed" error**
- Make sure "Install unknown apps" is enabled for the app you used to open the file (see Step 2)
- If you had an older version installed via a different method, uninstall it first and try again

**The app opens but shows no data**
- Check that your phone has an active internet connection
- The app needs internet to load tides, weather, and map tiles

**Tides show "too far from ocean"**
- This is expected if your selected location is more than 15 miles from the nearest NOAA tide station
- Try tapping the map on or near a coastal location

**The map is blank or shows a grey screen**
- This usually means your internet connection is too slow to load the map tiles — try on WiFi

**GPS isn't working**
- Go to Settings → Apps → CurrentCast → Permissions → Location and make sure it's set to "Allow while using app"

---

If you run into anything not covered here, open an issue on GitHub and describe what you're seeing — include your phone model and Android version.
